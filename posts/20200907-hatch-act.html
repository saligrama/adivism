A Brief Look at the Hatch Act (and RNC 2020 ethics violations)

Posted September 7, 2020

Previous Next

Post caption

When the 2020 Republican National Convention featured speeches set at the White House and by a sitting Secretary of State abroad on official business, it was widely panned for its violation of the Hatch Act and the shattering of ethics norms.

But on August 27, 2020, White House Chief of Staff Mark Pompeo said in an interview that“nobody outside of the [Washington, D.C.] Beltway really cares.”

Pompeo and the Trump administration evidently believe that ethics and laws do not matter to the American people. So, let’s explore the Hatch Act and these ethics norms that were clearly violated.
