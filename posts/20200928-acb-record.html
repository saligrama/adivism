Judge Amy Coney Barrett of the Seventh Circuit Court of Appeals was recently nominated to the Supreme Court after Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg’s passing.

Barrett’s opinions reveal a concerning lack of compassion for the powerless in front of her court as well as a propensity to favor excessive bureaucracy that disadvantages these powerless.

Let’s look at these worrying decisions written by the judge that may become the next Supreme Court justice.
