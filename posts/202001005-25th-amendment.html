A Brief Look at the 25th Amendment (and presidential succession)

Posted October 5, 2020

Previous Next

Post caption

With recent, concerning reports about the medical condition of President Trump, the U.S. has a number of safeguards in place to protect the office of the presidency in the case of his inability to serve.

Let’s take a look at these safeguards, including the 25th Amendment to the Constitution and the Presidential Succession Act of 1947.
