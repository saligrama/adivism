A Brief Look at Democracy Reform

Posted September 21, 2020

Previous Next

Post caption

While the American Experiment has represented a good first draft of a democratic system, it has become clear in recent years that our political system has serious flaws that endanger the execution of the will of the people.

But many reforms are realistically achievable without constitutional amendments, which would require action in 38 of 50 states.

In this post, we look at a set of modifications that could drastically improve our democratic system that only require Congressional and presidential action.
