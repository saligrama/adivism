A Brief Look at Affirmative Action

Posted June 29, 2020

Previous Next

Post caption

Affirmative action is a vital tool that businesses, universities, and the government use to maintain diversity of backgrounds and perspectives in the workplace and the classroom.

Let’s look at why affirmative action continues to be needed in 2020 and clear up some misconceptions that surround it.
