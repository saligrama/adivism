A Brief Look at the Dark Side of Chinese Tech

Posted July 20, 2020

Previous Next

Post caption

Recently, China has invested significantly in its tech industry, and Chinese conglomerates have in turn started to invest in foreign tech companies and game studios. Unlike within the US and other, freer nations, Chinese businesses must continuously acquiesce to the will of the Chinese government, including handing over data they collect and suppressing dissent.

As these corporate policies are often in effect globally, Chinese tech gives the Communist Party (CCP) the ability to enforce its domestic policy outside its borders.

In this post, we look at examples of how the Chinese government has used corporations as a proxy to curtail privacy and free speech abroad and prevent people from speaking out against its human rights abuses.
